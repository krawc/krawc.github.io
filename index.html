Konrad Krawczyk is a researcher of feedback loops between data, infrastructures, ecosystems and human communities. After having graduated from NYU Shanghai in 2019, he has worked for its Interactive Media Arts department, as well as for a host of Europe and US-based tech and media companies. In his research work, Konrad aims to go beyond critical theory into design of user interfaces and cloud applications.
